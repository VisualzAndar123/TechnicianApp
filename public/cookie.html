Cookie Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites or use web-based applications. They help the application remember information about your visit and preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

TechnicianApp uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our app
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for the app to function properly. These include authentication cookies and security cookies. Session or Persistent
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience. Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how users interact with our app to improve performance and features. Up to 2 years
Third-Party Cookies Set by our service providers (Firebase, Google) for authentication and functionality. Varies by provider

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Firebase (Google): For user authentication and data storage
  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics (if implemented)
  • Crashlytics: For error reporting and app stability

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

5.2 Mobile App Settings

Within the TechnicianApp, you can adjust certain privacy settings that affect how cookies are used.

5.3 Opt-Out Options

For analytics cookies, you may have opt-out options through your device settings or within the app.

6. Your Consent

By using TechnicianApp, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You will be presented with a consent banner on first use where required by law.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Our app does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers. We are working to implement this feature in future updates.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our services evolve. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email.
